GradeGrind Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Educational Data Protection

We comply with FERPA and other educational privacy regulations to protect student information and academic records.

Learning Analytics

We collect study patterns, progress data, and performance metrics to provide personalized learning recommendations.

AI Training Data

Anonymous usage data may be used to improve our AI algorithms, but personal identifiers are never included.

Third-Party Integration

Integration with learning management systems is done with explicit permission and follows institutional data policies.

Data Retention

Academic progress data is retained to maintain learning continuity, with options for data export and deletion upon request.
